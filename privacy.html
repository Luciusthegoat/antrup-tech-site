Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

Antrup Tech ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website antruptech.com and provides AI voice receptionist services to dental practices. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide

When you contact us for a demo or sign up for our services, we collect:

  • Your name and email address
  • Your dental practice name, address, and phone number
  • Billing information (processed securely through our payment provider)

Information collected automatically

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect:

  • IP address and browser type
  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • Referring URL

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our AI voice receptionist service
  • Communicate with you about your account and our services
  • Respond to your inquiries and demo requests
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Call Data & Recording

Our AI voice receptionist processes phone calls in real time. We may store:

  • Call transcripts for quality assurance and service improvement
  • Appointment booking data passed to your calendar system

We do not sell call data, transcripts, or patient information. Call data is stored securely and retained only as long as necessary to provide the service.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service providers who assist us in operating our business (hosting, payment processing, telephony)
  • Your dental practice management software (calendar integration, as authorized by you)
  • Law enforcement or regulatory authorities when required by law

5. HIPAA Compliance

Our service is designed to handle patient information in compliance with HIPAA requirements. We enter into Business Associate Agreements (BAAs) with each practice we serve. Patient health information is encrypted in transit and at rest.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption, access controls, and regular security reviews to protect your information. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, contact us at main@antruptech.com.

8. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies for essential functionality and analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
